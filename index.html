ABOUT US

We have created Stars Within Reach to animate a global interest in Space faring and to found a movement for young women to actively participate in the journey to space. Together, with the Mars-City Organization, we have begun a expedition into space so that we can look back down, heal the planet and create a new generation of young women designers and innovators. We are doing this via discussion groups all over the world. These groups will become friendship and knowledge exchange groups and will begin the important dialogue that will help us understand the new technologies that are here and coming; Graphene, living architecture, ion drive, satelites in space that can detect global warming and building a city on Mars. Our goal is to grow, from the exchange of knowledge and friendship, a new generation of dreamers and explorers.

Our Mission

"Our goal is to develop a platform where girls who don't have the same opportunities can connect with stay-at-home mothers as well as STEM professionals for a personalized and informative experience in the realm of education!"


To Empower

We are about girls looking up into the sky so they can see themselves in the future. We ignite dreams and help propell creativity so that girls become the designers and innovators of their generation. We want to give girls the oppurtunities that they most certainly deserve.


To Inform

Without specific knowledge of technological change, we can't join the future. We begin the discussion on becoming a multi-planet species which begins the dialogue to heal the planet. All earthlings needs to know about the problems that girls face, we need to work together to solve them.


To Create

We want to create a platform where young girls can connect and support each other. We want to build a global togetherness that we will use to build great civilizations in space and to solve the problems we face in our society today. We will connect the world, one star at a time.

3D Printing Food!

Imagine waking up in the morning, going to the kitchen and getting an instant breakfast from your 3D printer. That dream is slowly becoming a reality. NASA is funding a project that will help turn a 3D printer into a “space-kitchen”. Astronauts will use the 3D printer on long space flights to “cook” their foods. Pre-cooked meals don’t last very long and aren’t sustainable for long space missions, but now there's a solution!
