MISSION

We have created a place where young girls can understand coming space innovation and design the future that is coming. SWR helps girls integrate into the world of ideas, giving them the self-confidence to build something great.
SWR wants to get young people excited about the future!
We are doing this to build a global earth community and a togetherness that we will use to build great civilizations in space.
SWR strives to be connected with each other, now more than ever.
