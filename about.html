ABOUT US

We have created Stars Within Reach to animate a global interest in Space faring and to create a movement for young women to actively participate in the journey to space. Together, with the Mars-City Organization, we have begun a expedition into space so that we can look back down, heal the planet and create a new generation of young women designers and innovators. We are doing this via discussion groups all over the world. These groups will become friendship and knowledge exchange groups and will begin the important dialogue that will help us understand the new technologies that are here and coming; Graphene, living architecture, ion drive, satelites in space that can detect global warming and building a city on Mars. Our goal is to grow, from the exchange of knowledge and friendship, a new generation of dreamers and explorers.
